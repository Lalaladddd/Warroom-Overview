**Warroom** is an interactive Virtual Reality tactical battlefield game built with Unity and the XR Interaction Toolkit.  
Players take on the role of a **giant commander**, placing miniature tanks and soldiers into a virtual battlefield, where AI-driven armies fight each other.  

The goal is to deliver an immersive **tabletop wargame experience in VR** â€” blending tactical strategy, sandbox creativity, and AI-driven combat.

- **Immersive VR Gameplay**

  - Use VR controllers to pick up, drag, and place miniatures on the battlefield.  

- **Augmented Environment**  
  - Integration with Unity Pass Through Camera API to scan and use real-world surroundings as the battlefield backdrop.  

- **AI Simulation**  
  - AI-controlled soldiers and tanks with tactical decision-making.  
  - Pathfinding, movement, and combat interactions.  
  - Ability to simulate entire battles between AI factions.  

- **Commander Abilities**  
  - Players can command miniatures directly during battles.  
  - Enter **God Mode** to destroy miniatures or the battlefield itself.  
  - Future update: take control of a miniature and fight within the battlefield.
